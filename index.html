Our Vision

Live on the edge, and make a difference

YOU WONT is a mobile platform. Friends challenge each other to do new things, and crowdfund cash rewards. The person who steps up to the challenge can either take the money or donate it to a charity of their choice.

Greek life organizations, non-profit cooporations, and influencer fangroups can compete each month to raise the most money. The winner gets to choose a charity and 50% of all YOU WONT profit is donated that organization.
